Frequently Ask Questions

What types of freight do you handle?

We provide a wide range of transportation services including dry van, reefer, and specialized freight. No matter the load, our team ensures safe and timely delivery.

Do you offer dispatch services?

Yes. Our professional dispatch team helps drivers maximize their earnings by finding the best-paying loads, managing routes, and providing 24/7 support without forced dispatch.

Do I need to sign a long term contract?

No. We offer flexible agreements with no forced commitments. Our focus is on building long-term partnerships through reliable service.

What equipment do you provide?

We work with a variety of equipment including dry vans,sprinter vans and reefers,. We can match your freight requirements with the right solution.

How do you ensure on-time delivery?

Our experienced drivers, efficient dispatch team, and advanced route planning allow us to minimize delays and deliver shipments on schedule.

How can I get started with your services?

Simply contact our team through the website or by phone. We’ll discuss your shipping needs and provide a tailored solution that fits your business.

What regions do you serve?

We provide nationwide long-haul trucking as well as local and regional delivery solutions. Our team can adapt routes based on your shipping needs.

How much do owner-operators take home?

Owner-operators earn up to 90% of the total load gross. We are fully transparent with no hidden fees or surprise deductions.
